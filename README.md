# AI-Enabled-Event-Permission-and-Attendance-System
## AIM
The aim of this project is to create a web-based system that automates the process for students who seek permission from their respective Faculty members and the Head of Department (HOD) to participate in a given event. This system reduces paperwork, saves time, and improves transparency and tracking of approval workflows.

---

## PROJECT OVERVIEW
This website acts as a centralized platform where **Students**, **Faculty**, **HODs**, and **Event Managers** can interact seamlessly. Each user role has defined responsibilities, ensuring a structured and secure approval process.

---

## PROCESS FLOW

### 1. Registration
A common registration portal will be provided for the following user roles:
- Students
- Faculty
- Heads of Department (HODs)
- Event Managers

During registration, users will be required to provide basic information such as:
- **Name**
- **Email ID**

---

### 2. System Setup

#### a. Student Module
Students will be required to complete their profile by providing:
- Personal photograph
- Biometric sample images (for verification purposes)
- University roll number

Once the profile is completed:
- Students can apply for permission to their respective Faculty members
- Applications are sent based on the subject they are enrolled in

---

#### b. Faculty Module
Faculty members will:
- Provide details of the subjects they are teaching
- Receive permission requests from students enrolled in their subjects
- Review and accept or reject student requests

---

#### c. HOD Module
The Head of Department (HOD) will:
- Approve Faculty members registered under the department
- Verify and approve the subjects assigned to each Faculty member
- Act as the final authority in the academic hierarchy
